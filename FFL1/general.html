OVERVIEW

Final Fantasy Legend (SaGa 1) is a non-linear RPG where you assemble a party of 4 characters and climb through 4 worlds to reach the Tower of God. What makes it unique is that each character class grows in a fundamentally different way — there is no shared "level up" system.

HUMAN

Stats only increase by consuming stat-boosting items (Strong, Agility, etc.). Can equip 8 items/weapons at once. Very predictable, powerful with good gear.

→ Full guide
MUTANT

Gains random stat increases and random abilities after each battle. Only 4 equipment slots. High MANA potential makes them the best spellcasters.

→ Full guide
MONSTER

Cannot equip items. Carries 4 meat slots. Eating 4 meats of the same type transforms you into that monster family, giving you their stats.

BATTLE SYSTEM

Combat is turn-based. All party members and all enemies act each round. Turn order is determined by the AGI stat — higher AGI acts first.

Actions per character

  • Attack — use equipped weapon; damage based on weapon's stat (STR/AGI/MANA)
  • Use Item — consume a potion, use an ability/spell, eat meat (monsters)
  • Flee — attempt to escape; success based on party vs. enemy AGI

Damage formulas

Physical: damage = rand(str × 2, str × 3) − target.def (min 1)
Magic: damage = rand(mana × 2, mana × 3) (ignores DEF)
Gun: damage = weapon.base_power × rand(0.8, 1.2) (largely ignores DEF)
Elemental: effective damage ×1.5 if enemy has weakness
Tip: Guns deal consistent damage regardless of the enemy's DEF stat. They are very effective against high-DEF enemies in the later game.

TIPS & STRATEGY

SAVE OFTEN

Mutants can randomly lose good abilities. Save after every battle that gives you a useful ability, and before any fight that might do so.

MEAT STRATEGY

For monsters: plan your transformation chain. High-level meat from strong enemies gives you stronger forms. Don't waste high-tier meat on weak forms.

GUNS LATE GAME

Gun damage ignores DEF. As enemies gain hundreds of DEF in World 4 and the Tower, guns become dramatically more effective than STR-based swords.

CRYSTAL BALLS

Don't sell Crystal Balls — they open the world gates. Some appear in dungeons, some are guarded by bosses. Explore thoroughly before moving on.
