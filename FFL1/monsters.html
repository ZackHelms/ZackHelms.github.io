ALL MONSTERS (ROM DATA)

All 200 entries from the ROM monster table. HP from 0x1B254 (2-byte LE). STR/DEF/AGI/MANA from 0x1AAE8 bytes 2–5 (9-byte stride). Gold from BCD table at 0x1B2A4 via lower nibble of stat byte 6. Values exceeding 99 are highlighted — 99 is a display cap in-game, not the stored value.
