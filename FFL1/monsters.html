HOW THE MONSTER CLASS WORKS

A Monster character cannot equip weapons or armor. Instead, it holds up to 4 meat slots. When you defeat an enemy, it may drop a meat item. Eating meats is how your monster character grows stronger — by transforming into different monster forms.

MEAT SLOTS

Your monster can hold up to 4 meat items at once. Meat is obtained by defeating wild monsters. The meat matches the family of the monster you defeated.

TRANSFORMATION

Eat all 4 meats of the same family to transform into that family. The tier of your new form depends on the meat level (hidden value, 1–20).

STATS ON TRANSFORM

When you transform, you inherit the base stats of your new monster form at the given tier. Your old form and all its stats are lost permanently.

MEAT LEVEL

Each meat has a hidden level (1–20). Higher-level monsters drop higher-level meat. High-level meat transforms you into a stronger tier of the target family.

TRANSFORMATION TABLE

FFL1 has 26 monster families. When you eat 4 meats of the same family, you become the form in that family at the meat's level. The 6 tiers per family range from weakest (tier 1) to strongest (tier 6).

Strategy: Always aim to eat the highest-level meat possible. Late-game monsters drop level 15–20 meat, transforming you into the max-tier form with stats exceeding 99 for STR/DEF/AGI.
