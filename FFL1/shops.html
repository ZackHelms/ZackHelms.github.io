Data source notice: Shop inventories are extracted from ROM (0x17D38–0x17E0F, 14 shops × 10 item IDs each). Item prices use the 6-digit BCD table at 0x17E10. Shop locations (world/town) are not yet extracted from the ROM and are not shown. Shop indices 1–14 correspond to RANDO's equipment_shop_addrs array.
