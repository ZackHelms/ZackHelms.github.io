MUTANT CLASS
STAT GROWTH · ABILITIES · MAX VALUES · STRATEGY

CLASS OVERVIEW

Mutants occupy a middle ground between Humans and Monsters. They grow naturally through battle but with an element of randomness. Their greatest strength is MANA growth — a Mutant can become an extraordinarily powerful spellcaster by the end of the game.

EQUIPMENT

4 equipment slots for weapons/items (no armor slots).

Cannot equip helmets, chest armor, gloves, or shoes.

ABILITIES

4 separate ability slots (powers like Fire, Punch, Heal).

Acquired randomly after battle. Overflow silently deletes one random existing ability.

STAT GROWTH

After each battle victory, each stat has a chance to increase by 1–2.

The chance is influenced by your actions during the battle.

STAT GAIN SYSTEM

After each victorious battle, the game checks each of a Mutant's stats and may increase them.

STATTRIGGERNOTES
HP Killing enemies Increases most reliably. Will steadily grow toward 999.
STR Using STR-based weapons (swords, hammers) Grows slowly. Rarely exceeds ~50 without deliberate grinding.
AGI Using AGI-based weapons (rapier, guns) Grows with rapier use. Useful for turn-order advantage.
MANA Using spells or MANA-based abilities Highest growth rate. Can reach 99+ (internal) easily.
DEF Surviving hits Grows passively. Moderate growth.
Chance of +1 to a stat per battle ≈ 15–40% depending on actions taken
Optimize MANA: Use spells every battle to maximize MANA growth. A late-game Mutant with MANA 99+ (internal ~150–200) casts spells that deal 300–600 damage — far exceeding what weapons can do.

The display cap of 99 for STR/DEF/AGI/MANA is visual only. Internal values continue to increase past 99 and continue to affect combat calculations. MANA past 99 still increases spell damage significantly.

ABILITY SYSTEM

Mutants have 4 ability slots that are separate from equipment. Abilities are learned randomly after battles — you have no control over which ability you receive. When a 5th ability would be acquired, one of the 4 existing abilities is deleted at random without any warning.

Warning: The game gives NO notification when an ability is lost. After every battle where you might gain an ability, immediately check your party's ability list. Save often to avoid losing rare abilities.

OPTIMAL MUTANT STRATEGY

EARLY GAME

Equip a rapier or bow to build AGI. Use any spell abilities to build MANA. Buy Cure/Heal items to compensate for lack of armor.

MID GAME

Prioritize MANA growth. Cast spells every turn even when not necessary. Keep Heal ability at all costs — save/reload to protect it.

LATE GAME

With MANA >99 internally, Flare deals 400–700 damage. This exceeds most weapons. Keep 3–4 offensive spells and Heal for sustainability.

ABILITY MANAGEMENT

After gaining a useful ability, save immediately. If the next battle randomly drops a junk ability and deletes your Heal, you can reload. This is not cheating — it's intended (use the in-game save rooms).
