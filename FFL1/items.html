ROM-EXTRACTED DATA ONLY
Data source: Names, type, uses from 0x14640; GP prices from 0x17E10 (6-digit BCD, items 0–127 only; 128+ are monster/NPC-only abilities with no GP). Weapon power and armor defense from item stat table at 0x1B700. Element from Y field (HIGH confidence for single-element values). Status cure items: X field bitmask confirmed — MEDIUM confidence for status names (inferred from item names; needs BGB). Numeric effect amounts for spells and stat-boost usable items not yet extracted.

Power from X field at 0x1B700. Element from Y field (1=fire, 2=ice, 4=elec, 8=poison). Swords use prefix byte 0xEC (renders ' in-game); other weapons are guns/ranged. Uses from AltUses field (byte +3); most swords=50, ∞=unlimited, 1=breaks after one use.

Items without a slot are passive elemental defenses (D-/E-items) — they appear in monster ability lists, not in player equipment slots. Extended element types (stone/para/weapon/quake) are MEDIUM confidence (inferred from item names).
