WEAPON USES

Most weapons have limited uses (typically 20–40 uses). When uses reach 0 the weapon breaks and disappears. Always carry backups.

GUNS VS SWORDS

Guns deal fixed damage ignoring DEF. Swords use STR − DEF formula. In World 3+ where enemies have very high DEF, guns dominate.

EQUIP RESTRICTIONS

Humans: all weapons + armor (8 slots).
Mutants: weapons only (4 slots).
Monsters: nothing — meat only.

SPECIAL WEAPONS

Glass Sword: 1 use, destroys any enemy instantly.
Excalibur: hits all enemies for massive damage.
Masamune: high damage + special crit rate.
